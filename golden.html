Golden Triangle

Duration: 5 nights & 6 days
Destinations Covered: Delhi – Agra - Jaipur

PACKAGE HIGHLIGHTS:

• Rickshaw ride at Jama Mosque of Delhi
• Visit of a holy shrine (Gurudwara Bangla Sahib)
• Elephant ride at Amber Fort, Jaipur (Subject to availability)

DETAILS:

Day 1: Pick up from your hotel or airport and departs for Agra on arrival check-in and relax. Later afternoon visit Agra fort than enjoy the best view of Taj mahal at sunset time from other side of river. Overnight stay in hotel.
Day 2: Early morning visit the world famous monument of Love 'Taj-Mahal at sun rise. After breakfast start to Jaipur. En-route visit Fatehpur sikari and Abhaneri .Upon arrival check in at hotel. Overnight stay in hotel.
Day 3: After breakfast, Enjoy Amber Fort. Amber is a fascinating blend of Hindu and Muslim architecture. Later visit the Maharaja's City Palace, Jantar mantar (the Observatory), Hawa Mahal or the Palace of winds. In evening visit one of the most admired market in the world Johari bazaar, Choti chopper (famous for handicrafts, jewelry, stones etc.). Overnight stay in hotel at Jaipur.
Day 4: After breakfast, continue to Delhi and in the evening you can go for the shopping.
Day 5: After breakfast, enjoy Qutub Minar, Humayun tomb, Gurudwara bangle sahib. Later enjoy old delhi Jama Masjid with a cycle rickshaw ride in old city area.
Day 6: Post breakfast, you will be dropped at airport for your flight back home.
